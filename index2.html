"Power BI Insights: Visualize, Analyze, Dominate."

"Welcome to the 'Data to Dashboard' challenge, a competition designed to test and enhance students' Power BI skills. Participants will collect data from online sources, clean and prepare it, and then use Power BI to create interactive dashboards that reveal key insights. This event focuses on practical application, allowing students to demonstrate their ability to use Power BI for data analysis, visualization, and effective communication of findings. Judges will evaluate the clarity, interactivity, and impact of the dashboards, as well as the students' presentation skills."
